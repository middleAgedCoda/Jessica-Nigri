Jessica Nigri was born August 5, 1989 in Reno, Nevada — but raised in Christchurch, New Zealand, her mother's hometown. She moved back to the United States at age 12. Settled in Phoenix, Arizona. Attended Desert Vista High School. Holds dual American and New Zealand citizenship.

She began cosplaying in 2009 purely as a hobby. At San Diego Comic-Con that summer, she wore a "Sexy Pikachu" costume. It went viral overnight. The convention doors that opened from that moment would never close again. By 2011, Microsoft and GameStop had hired her as Anya Stroud to promote Gears of War 3.

She didn't follow the industry. She arrived before it knew what it was — and spent 16 years proving that cosplay is craft, art, voice, and culture simultaneously.
@jessicanigri · 4M Instagram ✅ · Cosplay Pioneer · Voice Actress · Reno, Nevada

In 2012, she won IGN's contest to portray Juliet Starling from Lollipop Chainsaw. That win earned her a spokesmodel contract with Warner Bros. Games — and a trip to Japan where she toured Famitsu, Dengeki, and Akihabara. The year she became international. In 2013, she voiced Cinder Fall in RWBY — carrying the role through RWBY Chibi and multiple volumes. That same year: Ubisoft, Suda51, and PAX. She was everywhere. She earned every room she walked into.

Voice Acting · RWBY · Rooster Teeth · Super Sonico · Red vs Blue

The voice
behind
the villain.

Since 2013, Jessica has voiced Cinder Fall in the Rooster Teeth animated series RWBY — one of the most iconic antagonist roles in Western anime-adjacent animation. She reprised the role through RWBY Chibi and multiple volumes of the main series.

She voiced Sonico in Super Sonico: The Animation in 2015 — a Japanese crossover that demonstrated her bilingual creative range. She has also appeared in Red vs. Blue, Rooster Teeth's landmark action comedy series.
